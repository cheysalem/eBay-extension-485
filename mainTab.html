How to Use Block-Bay Chrome Extension

Overview: Block-Bay is a Chrome extension designed to help you manage and control your online experience by allowing you to create a blacklist of sellers. Follow these simple steps to make the most of the extension.

Step 1: Open the Extension

Click on the Block-Bay icon in your Chrome browser's toolbar.

Step 2: Access Blacklist Editor

Once the extension is open, locate and click on the "Edit Blacklist" button.

Step 3: Edit Your Blacklist

You will see a section labeled "Blacklist Editor."

Each seller is represented by a row containing input fields.

  • To add a seller, click the "Add another seller to the Blacklist" button.
  • Enter the seller's name in the "Seller" input field.
  • Select the action you want for this seller from the "Select Type" dropdown menu (e.g., "Remove" or "Blur").
  • To remove a seller, click the "Remove seller" button next to the seller you want to remove.

Make sure to click the "Save" button to apply your changes.

Step 4: Save Your Blacklist

After making your desired changes, click the "Save" button to save your updated blacklist.

A status message will briefly appear, confirming that your options were saved.

Additional Tips:

  • To collapse the Blacklist Editor, click the "Edit Blacklist" button again.
  • If you accidentally remove all sellers, do not click save and add new seller. Simply reload the page to start fresh.

Enjoy a customized online experience with Block-Bay!
